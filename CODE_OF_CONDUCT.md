# Code of Conduct

Firefly Framework has adopted the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its Code of Conduct.

All participants in the Firefly Framework community are expected to follow it. Please read the full text at the link above.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing **conduct@fireflyframework.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate.
